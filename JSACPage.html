My Summers as a Sailing Instructor

I work at Jamestown Sea Adventure Camp as a Sailing Instructor!

The Camp is based out of a campground in Jamestown RI, where we have access to a boat ramp, a massive field for games, and a trailer to hold all of the instructor gear and any parts neccessary to maintain/fix the boats.

My responsibilites at the camp include, however are not limited to:

  1. Teach a group of 4-6 campers the fundamentals of sailing
  2. Be a leader for all campers
  3. Making sure all campers are included and are hvaing fun
  4. Maintence of the boats
  5. Handling all medical emergenices
  6. Lead all the games including kickball, soccer, 4 square, etc.
